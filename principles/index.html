Principles

An evolving list of beliefs and rules of thumb.

Last updated: March 2025.

  • Manage what you can control.
  • Trust yourself and be resourceful. Action is a choice.
  • Learn how to ask for help, even when you feel you do not need it.
  • Action produces information. Go and see for yourself.
  • Combine data with hunch-driven decisions. Be mindful of sample size; small or biased samples lead to bad decisions.
  • Sloppy preparation and sloppy decision-making are never excusable when they are controllable.
  • Prioritize sleep.
  • Exercise regularly.
  • Eat less and healthier. Drink more water.
  • Avoid sugar and alcohol.
