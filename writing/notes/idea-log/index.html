Sunday Mornings

Published on Jan 6, 2026

I love Sunday mornings. This is the only time when the world is truly still.

Imagine, you are watching a movie and then need to pause, so you can get some snacks. This paused scene in the movie is a Sunday morning - just place yourself there and think about being the only person able to move. And everything else is still.

This is how I feel every Sunday morning, just around the time when the day starts to break.
